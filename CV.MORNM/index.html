About 自己紹介

// 001

Hi — I'm Murtadha.

A student of multimedia from Baghdad. I design posters, cut videos, and slowly poke around code. Nothing fancy — I just like making things look nice.

I grew up on anime, and a lot of how I think about color, framing, and timing leaked in from those opening sequences I used to rewind too many times.

「

If a frame can stop someone for a second — it did its job.
